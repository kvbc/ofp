\hypertarget{getting_started_autotoc_md5}{}\doxysection{Options}\label{getting_started_autotoc_md5}
Options are either \href{argument_8h.html\#af82303803d7ccdedb90b343ba5316a59aacf611661ed01b51ba6328e589e6f1da}{\texttt{ Double-\/\+UIA}} ({\bfseries{DUIA}}) or \href{argument_8h.html\#af82303803d7ccdedb90b343ba5316a59a1fc4a8c449a1d725043d6abc7f64847f}{\texttt{ Single-\/\+UIA}} ({\bfseries{SUIA}}).

{\bfseries{DUIA}} options consist of two UIA, first one is the UDA identifier and the second one is the UDA value. \begin{quote}
{\ttfamily /option value} \end{quote}
{\bfseries{SUIA}} options consist of one UIA, including the UDA identifier and the UDA value. \begin{quote}
{\ttfamily /optionvalue} \end{quote}
